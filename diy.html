Advantages of Installing a Mini-Split AC Over Central AC

  • Lower Installation Costs: The cost of installing a central AC in the LA metro area ranges from $23,000 to $30,000. In contrast, mini-split systems are significantly cheaper, often costing between $2,000 and $7,000 per unit.
  • Energy Efficiency: Mini-split systems offer higher energy efficiency due to their inverter technology, which adjusts the compressor speed to maintain the desired temperature, leading to lower electricity bills.
  • Zone Cooling: Mini-split ACs allow for zone cooling, meaning you can cool only the rooms you use, avoiding the energy waste of cooling the entire home.
  • Reduced Energy Loss: Central AC systems can lose energy through the ductwork. Mini-split systems have no ducts, which eliminates this energy loss and increases overall efficiency.
  • Flexible Installation: Mini-split systems can be installed in various locations without the need for extensive ductwork, making them ideal for homes without existing ducts or those with limited space.
  • Quiet Operation: Mini-split units are typically quieter than central AC systems, providing a more comfortable living environment.
  • Individual Temperature Control: Each indoor unit of a mini-split system can be controlled independently, allowing for customized comfort in different rooms.
  • Lower Maintenance Costs: Mini-split systems generally require less maintenance than central AC systems, which need regular duct cleaning and more extensive servicing.
